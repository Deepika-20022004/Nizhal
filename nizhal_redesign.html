The Genesis & Origin

Our Foundation Story

How experience in counseling broken families led to a dedicated home for girl children in Coimbatore.

A Mission Born Out of Empathy

Nizhal Charitable Trust is a non-profitable, non-religious, and purely public-funded organization registered under the Indian Charitable Trust Act (No: 470/IV/11) in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu.

Our story began directly because of our professional careers. As legal and family consultancy experts operating the Global Solutions family consultancy clinic in Coimbatore since 2006, we handled hundreds of complex divorce, separation, and domestic dispute cases.

Every single day, we bore witness to the tragic, silent victims of these broken homes: the children. We saw young girls left depressed, highly vulnerable, and entirely abandoned due to the sudden separation, illness, or loss of their parents.

We realized that legal advice and moral support weren't enough. These children needed a secure roof, nutritious meals, structured educational support, and most of all, a place of belonging.

Thus, in 2011, Nizhal Charitable Trust was born. We originally provided support to both boys and girls with our own personal savings. Later, to strictly match the protection frameworks of the Government, from May 20, 2013, we restructured our focus exclusively on safeguarding and empowering destitute girl children aged 6 to 16.

"Our ultimate vision is to build confidence so these girls can grow up to take their rightful place in modern society as highly independent, educated, and valuable citizens."

Our Vision

To provide destitute, parentless, and abused girl children with a safe home, nutritious clothing, and robust formal schooling. We create an environment that is secure, fun, and creative where children can express themselves freely and heal.

Our Mission

To maintain a clean and highly supervised home where girl children receive hot wholesome food three times daily, full pairs of school uniforms, notebooks, specialized after-school evening tuition, and medical checkups completely free of cost.

Our Facilities & Commitments

The Core Pillars of Nizhal Care

Detailed standards we maintain on-site in Goundampalayam to secure the psychological and physical growth of the children.

Study Hour (Evening Tuition)

We pay specialized attention to our children's education. A dedicated home tutor visits our premises every evening to guide the girls with homework, clarify core school concepts, and prepare them for examinations.

Wholesome Daily Nutrition

Meals are freshly prepared inside our hygienic home kitchen. Children receive hot breakfast, tea, lunch, nutritious evening snacks, and freshly prepared dinner consisting of healthy grains and seasonal local vegetables.

Clothes, Uniforms & Footwear

We ensure our girls never feel left behind. Every child receives multiple sets of premium casual clothes, school uniforms, matching shoes, writing books, pens, school bags, and personal toiletries.

Pediatric Health Monitoring

Periodical medical checkups are arranged with visiting local pediatricians. Height, weight, vaccine intervals, and general nutritional requirements are rigorously tracked for every girl's growth curve.

Cultural Arts & Music

Weekend schedules are structured to build artistic expression. The girls participate in classical singing lessons, drawing, crafting, and folk dancing to build cognitive confidence and relieve traumatic distress.

Recreation & Playtime

Physical play is crucial for sound mental development. Our home features secure play fields where girls enjoy playing badminton, skipping-ropes, and traditional Indian group games with administrative staff supervision.
