In his own words

Founder’s Message

First of all, my hearty thanks for visiting our website. My wife and I started this organisation to help and serve destitute and abused children — the “forgotten” members of our society.

I run a legal and family-welfare consultancy, where every day I handle cases — many of them divorces — and counsel children who have become depressed and insecure after the sudden loss of a parent, whether to an accident or other means.

“These children have lost so much, yet their hearts are filled with love and hope for the future. Together, we can return that love — by giving hope, education, love and protection.”

This is what moved us to open Nizhal Home — to shelter and serve children facing the harsh realities of poverty, to ease their hardship, and to help them live happy, peaceful lives. I ask everyone reading this to join us in that effort.

— R. Rajagopal, Founder & Trustee

Nizhal

A non-profit, non-religious home in Coimbatore caring for destitute and abused girls since 2011 — providing shelter, education and the steadiness of a family.
