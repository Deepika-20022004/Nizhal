Nizhal

A home, and a measure of shade, for girls the world overlooked

Nizhal Charitable Trust is a non-profit, non-religious home in Coimbatore caring for destitute and abused girls — providing shelter, education, nutrition and the steadiness of a family, since 2011.

What we provide

A day at Nizhal

  • Shelter & daily care

    Lodging, boarding and every day amenities for every child who comes through our door.

  • School & tutoring

    Uniforms and stationery, and a qualified tutor for remedial study after school.

  • Nutrition & health

    Wholesome meals and regular medical check-ups, for body and mind alike.

  • Play, sport & craft

    Daily exercise, sport and creative activities, plus special programmes for festivals.
