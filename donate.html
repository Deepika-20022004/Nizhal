Around 20 girls and 2 staff (cook and warden) currently live at Nizhal. Caring for them — rent, salaries, school transport, books, food, medical care — costs roughly ₹60,000 a month, about ₹2,000 a day. Every contribution, large or small, reaches a child directly.
